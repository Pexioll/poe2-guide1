# Act 4

Final boss: **Tavakai, the Chieftain**

> **Tip:** Three out of the first four islands are essential for the main quest, but the required islands will change between leagues. One of these four islands also holds Matiki, who needs to be saved to access Eye of Hinekora. The island boss rewards also change between leagues. The optional bosses may be skipped.

## Tasks

1. Town (Kingsmarch) - Talk to NPCs for Book Charter - Talk to Makoru - Sail to Kedge Bay.
2. Kedge Bay - Go to Journey's End.
3. Journey's End - Summon Tujen - Kill **Captain Hartlin** (end of zone) - Take Verisium - Portal to Town.
4. Town - Talk to Dannig for Verisium Spikes - WP to Journey's End.
5. Journey's End - Talk to Freya - Activate Karui Totems - Kill **Omniphobia, Fear Manifest** *(2 skillpoints)* - Portal to Town.
6. Town - Talk to Tujen *(2 skillpoints)* - Talk to Makoru - Sail to Isle of Kin.
7. Isle of Kin - Kill **The Blind Beast** *(optional)* - Go to Volcanic Warrens.
8. Volcanic Warrens - Kill **Krutog, Lord of Kin** (end of zone) - Talk to Hooded One - Return to Ship.
9. Ship - Talk to Makoru - Sail to Abandoned Prison.
10. Abandoned Prison - Kill monsters for Chapel Key - Open Chapel Door - Tag WP - Activate Goddess of Justice *(Flask recovery, can be changed)* - Use Levers - Go to Solitary Confinement.
11. Solitary Confinement - Use Levers - Kill **The Prisoner** - Talk to Hooded One - Return to Ship.
12. Ship - Talk to Makoru - Sail to Whakapanu Island.
13. Whakapanu Island - Go to Singing Caverns.
14. Singing Caverns - Tag WP - Kill **Diamora, Song of Death** - Talk to Hooded One - Return to Ship.
15. Ship - Talk to Makoru - Sail to Shrike Island.
16. Shrike Island - Kill **Scourge of the Skies** (end of zone) - Talk to Hooded One - Return to Ship.
17. Ship - Talk to Makoru - Sail to Eye of Hinekora.
18. Eye of Hinekora - Talk to NPCs - Activate Well of Hinekora - Pass 3 tests - Click Pay your Respects *(Mana boost)* - Go to Halls of the Dead (further down).
19. Halls of the Dead - Tag WP - Pass Tawhoa's test *(Dex/Lightning Resistance)* - Pass Tasalio's test *(Int/Cold Resistance)* - Pass Ngamahu's test *(Str/Fire Resistance)* - Defeat **Yama The White** - Take Silver Coin - Go to Trial of the Ancestors.
20. Trial of the Ancestors - Tag WP - Talk to Navali - Take Tattoo of Hinekora *(2 skillpoints)* - Return to Ship.
21. Ship - Talk to Makoru - Sail to Arastas.
22. Arastas - Tag WP - Talk to Missionary Lorandis - Enter church, Exit the church, Destroy forcefield - Kill **Torvian, Hand of the Saviour** - Go to Excavation.
23. Excavation - Kill **Benedictus, First Herald of Utopia** - Enter forge - Talk to Hooded One - Portal to Town.
24. Town - Talk to NPCs - Talk to Rhodri (at the ship) - Sail to Ngakanu.
25. Ngakanu - Go to Heart of the Tribe.
26. Heart of the Tribe - Tag WP - Kill **Tavakai, the Chieftain**, **Tavakai, the Fallen** and **Tavakai, the Consumed** - Portal to Town.
27. Town - Talk to NPCs - Talk to Hooded One - Go to Ogham, Vastiri, or Mount Kriar.
