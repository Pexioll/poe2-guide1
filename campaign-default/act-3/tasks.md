# Act 3

Final boss: **Doryani, Royal Thaumaturge**

## Tasks

1. Sandswept Marsh - Go to Ziggurat Encampment.
2. Town (Ziggurat Encampment) - Talk to NPCs - Go to Jungle Ruins (top).
3. Jungle Ruins - Kill **Mighty Silverfist** *(2 skillpoints)* - Go to Venom Crypts (center of zone near WP).
4. Venom Crypts - Take Corpse-snake Venom from Corpse (end of zone) - Portal to Town.
5. Town - Talk to Servi *(Various boosts, cannot be changed)* - WP to Jungle Ruins - Go to Infested Barrens (opposite from WP).
6. Infested Barrens - Tag WP - Summon Alva - Go to Azak Bog.
7. Azak Bog - Tag WP - Summon Servi - Kill **Ignagduk, the Bog Witch** *(30 Spirit bonus)* - Take Ignagduk's Ghastly Spear - Portal to Town.
8. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Infested Barrens - Go to Chimeral Wetlands (opposite Azak Bog).
9. Chimeral Wetlands - Tag WP - Kill **Xyclucian, the Chimera** - Take Chimeral Inscribed Ultimatum - Go to Jiquani's Machinarium (within boss arena).
10. Jiquani's Machinarium - Tag WP - Summon Alva - Take Small Soul Core - Activate Stone Altar - Take Small Soul Cores & Activate Stone Altars - Kill **Blackjaw, the Remnant** *(Fire Resistance boost)* - Go to Jiquani's Sanctum.
11. Jiquani's Sanctum - Tag WP - Summon Alva - Find 3 Medium Soul Cores & Start 2 Generators (corners of zone) - Activate Large Soul Core near Alva - Kill **Zicoatl, Warden of the Core** - Take Large Soul Core - WP to Infested Barrens.
12. Infested Barrens - Activate Stone Altar (near WP) - Go to Matlan Waterways (nearby).
13. Matlan Waterways - Pull all the Canal Levers - Pull the large Canal Lever (end of zone) - Portal to Town.
14. Town - Go down the stairs - Talk to Alva - Go to Drowned City.
15. Drowned City - Tag WP - Summon Oswald - Go to Molten Vault.
16. *(Once per league)* Molten Vault - Tag WP - Use Lever - Pull Sluice Gate Lever - Kill **Mektul, the Forgemaster** *(Reforging Bench)* - Take The Hammer of Kamasa - Talk to Oswald - Portal to Town.
17. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Drowned City - Go to Apex of Filth.
18. Apex of Filth - Tag WP - Kill **Queen of Filth** (end of zone) - Take Temple Door Idol - Portal to Town.
19. Town - Go down the stairs - Talk to Alva - Open Door - Go to Temple of Kopec.
20. Temple of Kopec - Climb Stairs twice (corner of pyramid) - Kill **Ketzuli, High Priest of the Sun** - Summon Alva, Investigate Platform - Enter gateway - Tag WP (behind gateway) - Go down stairs - Go to Utzaal.
21. Utzaal - Tag WP - Kill **Viper Napuatzi** - Kill monsters for Sacrificial Heart - Go to Aggorat.
22. Aggorat - Tag WP - Go to altar - Take Sacrificial Dagger - Place and stab Sacrificial Heart *(2 skillpoints)* - Go to Black Chambers.
23. Black Chambers - Tag WP - Kill **Doryani, Royal Thaumaturge** and Doryani's Triumph - Talk to Doryani - Portal to Town - Talk to Doryani for apocalypse.
24. Town - Talk to NPCs - Talk to Alva - Go to Act 4.
