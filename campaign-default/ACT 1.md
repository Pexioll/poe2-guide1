# Act 1

Final boss: **Count Geonor**

## Tasks

1. Kill **Bloated Miller** - Use skillpoint - Go to Clearfell Encampment.
2. Town (Clearfell Encampment) - Talk to NPCs - Go to Clearfell.
3. Clearfell - Kill **Beira of the Rotten Pack** *(Cold Resistance bonus)* - Go to Grelwood.
4. Grelwood - Tag WP - Summon Una (near WP) - Go to Red Vale (opposite side).
5. Red Vale - Activate 3 Obelisks of Rust & Kill **The Rust King** - Take Runed Girdle, Runed Guard and Runed Skull Cap - Portal to Town.
6. Town - Talk to NPCs for Runed Spikes - WP to Grelwood.
7. Grelwood - Activate 3 Runic Seals - Portal to Town.
8. Town - Talk to NPCs - Go to Grelwood.
9. Grelwood - Go to Grim Tangle (center of zone) - Summon Una - Go to Cemetery of the Eternals.
10. Cemetery - Talk to Lachlann - Enter Mausoleum of the Praetor - Kill **Draven, the Eternal Praetor** - Take Draven's Memorial Key Piece - Go to Cemetery.
11. Cemetery - Enter Tomb of the Consort (opposite WP) - Kill **Asinia, the Praetor's Consort** - Take Asinia's Memorial Key Piece - Go to Cemetery.
12. Cemetery - Talk to Lachlann - Kill **Lachlann of Endless Lament** - Take Count Lachlann's Ring - Portal to Town.
13. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Hunting Grounds.
14. Hunting Grounds - Talk to Delwyn - Kill **The Crowbell** *(2 skillpoints)* - Go to Freythorn.
15. Freythorn - Complete all rituals - Kill **The King in the Mists** *(30 Spirit bonus)* - Talk to Finn - Portal to Town.
16. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Hunting Grounds - Go to Ogham Farmlands.
17. Ogham Farmlands - Tag WP - Take Una's Lute from Una's Lute Box (before farms) - Go to Ogham Village.
18. *(Once per league)* Ogham Village - Tag WP - Take **Smithing Tools** *(salvage bench)* - Portal to Town - Talk to Renly - Return to Ogham Village.
19. Ogham Village - Kill **The Executioner** *(end of zone)* *(2 skillpoints)* - Use Lever - Talk to Leitis - Go to Manor Ramparts.
20. Manor Ramparts - Tag WP - Go to Town - Talk to NPCs *(2 skillpoints)* - WP to Manor Ramparts - Go to Ogham Manor.
21. Ogham Manor - Kill **Candlemass, the Living Rite** *(Life bonus)* - Go down stairs to Arena - Kill **Count Geonor** - Portal to Town.
22. Town - Talk to NPCs - Talk to Hooded One - Go to Act 2.
