# Act 2

Final boss: **Jamanra, the Abomination**

## Tasks

1. Vastiri Outskirts - Kill **Rathbreaker** - Portal to Town - Talk to Zarka - Go to Ardura Caravan.
2. Town (Ardura Caravan) - Talk to NPCs - Desert Map to Mawdun Quarry.
3. Mawdun Quarry - Go to Mawdun Mine.
4. Mawdun Mine - Tag WP - Kill **Rudja, the Dread Engineer** - Open Cage - Talk to Risu - Portal to Town.
5. Town - Talk to NPCs - Desert Map to Halani Gates.
6. Halani Gates - Talk to Asala - Go to Town.
7. Town - Talk to NPCs - Desert Map to Traitor's Passage.
8. Traitor's Passage - Tag WP - Activate Ancient Seal (middle of zone) - Activate 3 Runic Seals - Kill **Balbala, the Traitor** - Take Balbala's Barya - Go to Halani Gates.
9. Halani Gates - Tag WP - Summon Asala - Defeat **Jamanra, the Risen King** - Go to sandstorm - Portal to Town.
10. Town - Talk to NPCs - Desert Map to Keth.
11. Keth - Kill serpents for Kabala Clan Relic - Kill **Kabala, Constrictor Queen** *(2 skillpoints)* - Go to Lost City.
12. Lost City - Go to Buried Shrines - Go to Heart of Keth.
13. Heart of Keth - Kill **Azarian, the Forsaken Son** - Talk to Water Goddess - Take Everburning Cinders - Ignite Water Goddess - Take The Essence of Water - Portal to Town.
14. Town - Talk to NPCs - Desert Map to Mastodon Badlands.
15. Mastodon Badlands - Go to Lightless Passage (center of zone) - Go to Well of Souls.
16. Well of Souls - Speak to Lurking Creature *(Reveal Desecrated mods)* - Speak to Sin - WP to Lightless Passage - Return to Mastodon Badlands.
17. Mastodon Badlands - Go to Bone Pits.
18. Bone Pits - Tag WP - Kill monsters for Sun Clan Relic - Go to Blackrib Pit - Kill **Iktab, the Deathlord** and **Ekbab, Ancient Steed** - Take Mastodon Tusks - Portal to Town.
19. Town - Talk to NPCs - Desert Map to Valley of Titans.
20. Valley of Titans - Tag WP - Activate Medallion near WP *(Charm bonus, can be changed)* - Activate 3 Ancient Seals around the zone - Enter Titan Grotto.
21. Titan Grotto - Kill **Zalmarath, the Colossus** - Take The Flame Ruby - Portal to Town.
22. Town - Talk to NPCs for The Horn of the Vastiri - Desert Map to Traitor's Passage - Go to the front of the caravan and Sound The Horn - Talk to NPCs - Desert Map to Deshar.
23. Deshar - Find Lailuma's Body (Fallen Dekhara) - Take Final Letter *(2 skillpoints)* - Kill **Hunin, Storm Caller** and **Mugin, Frost Bringer** - Take Djinn Barya - Go to Path of Mourning.
24. Path of Mourning - Go to Spires of Deshar.
25. Spires of Deshar - Tag WP - Activate Sisters of Garukhan shrine *(Lightning Resistance boost)* - Kill **Tor Gul, the Defiler** - Portal to Town.
26. Town - Talk to NPCs *(2 skillpoints)* - Desert Map to Dreadnought.
27. Dreadnought - Go to Dreadnought Vanguard - Tag WP - Kill **Jamanra, the Abomination** - Portal to Town.
28. Town - Talk to NPCs - Talk to Asala - Go to Act 3.
