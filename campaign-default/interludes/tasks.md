# Interludes

> **Tip:** You can complete the interlude areas in any order.

---

## Ogham

1. Town (The Refuge) - Talk to NPCs - Go to Scorched Farmlands (bottom exit).
2. Scorched Farmlands - Kill **Heldra of the Black Pyre** and **Isolde of the White Shroud** - Go to Stones of Serle.
3. Stones of Serle - Activate all Runed Megaliths - Go to center rune - Kill **Siora, Blade of the Mists** - Talk to Una - Go to Scorched Farmlands.
4. Scorched Farmlands - Go to darkness - Go to Blackwood - Go to Holten.
5. Holten - Go to Wolvenhold.
6. Wolvenhold - Kill **Oswin, the Dread Warden** *(2 skillpoints)* - Go to Holten - Go to Holten Estate.
7. Holten Estate - Tag WP - Go upstairs, then downstairs - Kill **Thane Wulfric** and **Lady Elswyth** (ground floor) - Portal to Town.
8. Town - Talk to NPCs - Talk to Hooded One - Go to Vastiri.

---

## Vastiri

1. Town (The Khari Bazaar) - Talk to NPCs - Go to Khari Crossing.
2. Khari Crossing - Kill **Akthi, the Final Sting** and **Anundr, the Sandworm** (top right) - Portal to Town.
3. Town - Talk to Risu *(2 skillpoints)* - Return to Khari Crossing.
4. Khari Crossing - Go to Skullmaw Stairway (top left) - Take Molten One's Gift *(Life boost)* - Return to Khari Crossing - Go to Pools of Khatal (bottom left).
5. Pools of Khatal - Tag WP - Go to Sel Khari Sanctuary.
6. Sel Khari Sanctuary - Tag WP - Kill **Elzarah, the Cobra Lord** - Talk to Asala - Portal to Town.
7. Town - Talk to NPCs - Go to Khari Crossing - Go to Galai Gates (left).
8. Galai Gates - Tag WP - Kill **Vornas, the Fell Flame** - Go to Qimah.
9. Qimah - Tag WP - Activate Seven Pillars *(Various boosts, can be changed)* - Summon Jado (end of zone) - Go to Qimah Reservoir.
10. Qimah Reservoir - Tag WP - Kill **Azmadi, the Faridun Prince** - Activate Grand Barya - Talk to Jado - Portal to Town.
11. Town - Talk to NPCs - Talk to Hooded One - Go to Mount Kriar.

---

## Mount Kriar

1. Town (The Glade) - Talk to NPCs - Go to Ashen Forest.
2. Ashen Forest - Go to Kriar Village.
3. Kriar Village - Tag WP - Kill **Lythara, the Wayward Spear** *(40 Spirit boost)* - Go to Glacial Tarn.
4. Glacial Tarn - Tag WP - Go to Howling Caves.
5. Howling Caves - Tag WP - Kill **The Abominable Yeti** *(2 skillpoints)* - Take Icy Tusks - Portal to Town.
6. Town - Talk to Hilda *(2 skillpoints)* - WP to Glacial Tarn.
7. Glacial Tarn - Kill **Rakkar, the Frozen Talon** - Go to Kriar Peaks.
8. Kriar Peaks - Tag WP - Take gift from Elder Madox - Go to Etched Ravine.
9. Etched Ravine - Tag WP - Kill **Stormgore, the Guardian** - Go to Cuachic Vault.
10. Cuachic Vault - Tag WP - Kill **Zelina, Blood Priestess** and **Zolin, Blood Priest** - Summon Doryani - Portal to Town.

---

## Endgame

1. WP to Kingsmarch (Act 4 town).
2. Kingsmarch - Talk to NPCs - Talk to Hooded One *(2 skillpoints)* - Travel to Oriath.
3. The Ziggurat Refuge - Talk to NPCs - Run one map in Ziggurat to unlock maps - Start mapping in hideout.
